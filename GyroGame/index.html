GyroGame

Please disable the auto-rotation functionality on your phone and press start to begin

How to

Tilt your phone to guide the hungry mouth towards the gyros, like a marble.
You get 10s for each gyro. Eat it before the time runs out, to generate the next one and reset the timer.
If you don't eat it quickly or go out of bounds you lose a life!
